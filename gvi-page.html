Goodwill Notes App

Semester Overview

This semester, our project team collaborated to develop a comprehensive notes application tailored for Goodwill. Our collective efforts resulted in a successful integration of the front-end and back-end systems, culminating in significant advancements across various front-end pages, meticulously crafted based on the detailed Figma wireframes. Employing Insomnia, we meticulously ensured the seamless transfer of accurate data and established a robust and reliable connection between different components of the application.

End of the semester reflection

As we conclude this semester, we're eagerly anticipating the opportunity to continue our work on this application in the upcoming semester. We are excited about delving deeper into the specific requirements outlined by the Goodwill Representative. This next phase will enable us to further enhance the application's functionalities, ensuring that it aligns seamlessly with the unique needs and objectives set forth by Goodwill.
